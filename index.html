Artist Statement

My analog art practice serves as a counterbalance to my digital work, providing a tactile connection to traditional creative processes. Through drawing, painting, and mixed media, I explore fundamental concepts of form, light, and composition that inform both my artistic vision and technical approach to design.

Working with traditional media requires patience, deliberation, and acceptance of imperfection‚Äîqualities that have deeply influenced my problem-solving approach in software development. The intersection of these practices creates a unique perspective that values both precision and organic discovery.
